Q-Dance
Q-Dance will bring the incomparable experience of the lion stage to austria and top it off with an amazing light and pyro show. More than 25 of the best hardstyle acts in the world will perform on the Q-Dance stage on friday july 10th and saturday july 11th. Exclusive Q-Dance VIP passes are available for an elevated deck next to the stage for an unforgettable VIP experience.

Entrance to the Q-Dance VIP area is also granted with a VIP Gold ticket (SOLD OUT). opening hours: Friday July 10th 12:00 - 03:00 (Music until 02:00) Saturday July 11th 12:00 - 03:00 (Music until 02:00)
