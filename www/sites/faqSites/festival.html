Festival

For information about the Electric Love Festival use our app "Electric Love" available for iOS, Android and other popular providers. It will be around in time for the festival.

For each sold festival ticket the Electric Love Festival donates € 1,00 to the Austrian Cancer Aid Association and supports its project "Mama, Papa hat Krebs" (engl. Mom, Dad has Cancer)

Visit the Electric Love charging station and take one of our Red Bull Energy Packs for a fee to recharge your mobile phone. After it is fully charged return the Energy Pack and get your deposit refunded.

There is a special currency at the festival called Electric Love Token which is used for buying food, drinks and merchandise. There are loads of possibilities to change your money (both cash and cashless) into token at declared token stands all over the festival site. If you have left over token there is the possibility to change them back into cash during the whole festival. The token bought in advance (Token Bag) have a different design and cannot be changed back due to the discount!

The Electric Love Festival conduct an absolute zero tolerance policy with regard to drugs! It is prohibited to use, deal or be in possession of drugs on both the festival grounds and the camping site.

There are several first aid tents all over the festival area. You can see their position on the map.

You´re hungry? That´s no problem. There are loads of different Electric Food Bars all over the area. Payment with token.
