Visitors from Austria

Autobahn (Highway) A1 from Vienna/Innsbruck/Salzburg

Exit Thalgau – route to Hof, in the roundabout first exit direction Salzburg. From there you´ll see information signs which will guide you to your destination. Please mind the different colors for the different areas.

Autobahn (Highway) A10 from Kärnten (Carinthia) / Italy

Exit Hallein – route to Adnet/Ebenau/Salzburgring. Just follow the road and you´ll be fine.

Visitors from Germany

With Vignette (road tax sticker on your car, can be bought on e.g. gas stations)

Autobahn (Highway) A8 from Munich to Autobahn (Highway) A1 route Vienna

Exit Thalgau – route to Hof, in the roundabout first exit direction Salzburg. From there you´ll see information signs which will guide you to your destination. Please mind the different colors for the different areas.

Without Vignette (road tax sticker on your car, can be bought on e.g. Gas stations)

Autobahn (Highway) A8 from Munich

Exit Piding – route Freilassing and further Salzburg when you pass the placename sign “Salzburg” you have to drive on the B 155, further to the B1. Watch out for the information sisgns and mind the different colors for the different areas.
