To realise our goal "the cleanest festival in Austria" we need your help. We will ask you for a deposit of € 15,00 at the camping entrance for which you will receive a garbage chip. If you bring back a fully filled trash bag and the chip to the exit the desposit will be given back to you. Trash bags are available at Info/Lost&Found Stands & the exit.
If you want to camp as a group please arrive as a group and accept that the security staff is not in charge to merge groups on camping site.
